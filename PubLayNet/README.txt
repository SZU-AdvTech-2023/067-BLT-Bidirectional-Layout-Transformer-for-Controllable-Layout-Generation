Folder Structure: 
The images are in 3 splits - test, train and val. Each in the corresponding folder.
-- train/
    --*.PNG 
-- test/
    --*.PNG
-- val/
    --*.PNG

The labels for train and val splits can be found in `train.json` and `val.json`. The labels are in the MS COCO format.
